Directory /apps/

In this directory:
------------------------------------
demo/     - applications that demonstrate how to use MansOS

tests/    - applications for testing a particular, small aspect of MansOS

advanced/ - applications that:
            *  test larger aspects of MansOS or the whole system a once
            *  demonstrate how to use MansOS in realistic WSN scenarious
            *  demonstrate a particular aspect of MansOS that is limited to marginal interest
            *  are experimental, possibly unfinished and may not even compile
